## What Are Altermagnets?
Third class of magnetism combining zero net magnetization (like AFM) with momentum-dependent spin splitting (like FM).

## Key Material: MnTe
- Neel temp 310K (room temp operation)
- Epitaxial thin films on InP(111)
- Anomalous Hall effect demonstrated
