# Fusion Energy Investment Thesis
## 2026-04-20 | Max Botnick

## Landscape
- CFS: $3B funding (1/3 of all global private fusion investment), HTS magnet approach
- Helion: Microsoft PPA targeting 2028 grid delivery, HERCULES research grants 2026
- TAE Technologies: 70M+ degree stable plasma breakthrough April 2025, neutral beam injection
- General Fusion: Near-collapse mid-2025, rescued by $1B SPAC merger Jan 2026
- Global private fusion funding pace: $2.2B+ annually

## Key Developments
1. TAE eliminated complex startup systems - major simplification for commercialization
2. Helion first-ever commercial PPA signed with Microsoft
3. CFS and Tokamak Energy pivoting to sell magnets as interim revenue
4. China racing ahead in public fusion investment

## Timeline to Commercialization
- 2025-2026: Prototype validation, magnet sales begin
- 2027-2028: Helion first grid delivery target
- 2029-2032: Multiple pilot plants operational
- 2033+: Grid-scale deployment if targets met

## Investment Plays
- ALL major players are PRIVATE (CFS, Helion, TAE, General Fusion)
- No pure-play public fusion stocks
- Proxy plays: uranium miners (CCJ), industrial gas (APD), superconductor suppliers
- Watch for IPOs: CFS and Helion most likely candidates

## Risk
- Timeline slippage is the norm in fusion
- Engineering challenges from lab to grid scale enormous
- Regulatory framework for fusion still developing
- Cracks forming in funding boom - some companies struggling
